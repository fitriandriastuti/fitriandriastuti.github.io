    ABOUT ME¶

    Hi, I'm Fitri Andri Astuti.

    Currently, I work as a Software Engineer, Data Scientist, Statistician at the Central Bureau of Statistics of the Republic of Indonesia. I obtained my Master of Engineering degree from the Bandung Institute of Technology, Indonesia. While working on my thesis in my master's degree, I was guided by Mr. I Gusti Bagus Baskara Nugraha, S.T., M.T., Ph.D.

    I have always wanted to have my own web blog to write about my experiences exploring the world of technology. This website is one of the things that I want to realize as one of my dreams to be able to share knowledge, because useful knowledge keeps flowing.
